About SpareBite

We are a food donation platform based in Johor Bahru, Malaysia, dedicated to reducing food waste while helping our local community.

Our mission is to connect generous food donors with those in need, creating a sustainable cycle of sharing that benefits everyone. Whether you're a restaurant with surplus meals, a grocery store with unsold items, or an individual with extra food, SpareBite makes it easy to share and make a difference.
